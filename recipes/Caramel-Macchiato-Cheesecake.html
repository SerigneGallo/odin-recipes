Caramel Macchiato Cheesecake

Description

Caramel Macchiato Cheesecake is a rich dessert featuring a creamy cheesecake base infused with espresso, topped with a generous swirl of caramel. The crust is usually a simple graham cracker base, complementing the sweet and slightly bitter coffee flavors.

This cheesecake is often garnished with whipped cream and additional caramel drizzle, making it a favorite for coffee lovers and a decadent treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
  • ¼ cup brewed espresso or strong coffee
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Pressurized whipped cream
  • Caramel ice cream topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven: Set the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9-inch springform pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Prepare the crust: Mix the graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 2 tablespoons of sugar until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom and 1 inch up the sides of the prepared springform pan. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 minutes, then remove and cool on a wire rack.
  3. Adjust oven temperature: Reduce the oven temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  4. Make the filling: In a large bowl, use an electric mixer to beat the softened cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually add 1 cup of sugar, beating until blended. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in sour cream, espresso, and vanilla extract. Pour the batter into the baked and cooled crust.
  5. Bake the cheesecake: Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour and 5 minutes; then turn the oven off, partially open the door and let the cheesecake rest inside for 15 more minutes. Remove from the oven, and carefully run a knife around the edges. Allow the cheesecake to cool on a wire rack to room temperature, then cover the springform pan with plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator for 8 hours.
  6. Serve: To serve, cut the cheesecake into wedges and garnish each slice with whipped cream and caramel sauce.
